## Key Insights

### ✅ Strengths

1. **High Recommendation Coverage**: 87.2% of registered visitors received personalized recommendations
2. **Strong Engagement Lift**: Visitors with recommendations attended sessions at 81.3% vs 60.2% without — a **+21.1 pp** absolute increase (**+35.1%** relative lift)
3. **Good Show Attendance**: 93% of registered visitors physically attended the event

### ⚠️ Areas for Improvement

1. **Recommendation Hit Rate**: Only 27.7% of visitors with recommendations attended a recommended session
2. **Coverage Gap**: 811 visitors (12.8%) did not receive recommendations
3. **Opportunity**: 3,984 visitors received recommendations but didn't attend any recommended sessions

### 🎯 Recommendations

1. **Improve relevance**: Analyze why 72.3% of visitors didn't attend recommended sessions
2. **Increase coverage**: Investigate why 811 visitors missed the recommendation cutoff
3. **Boost conversion**: Consider push notifications or reminders for recommended sessions
