## Key Insights

### ✅ Strengths

1. **High Registration Campaign Re-registration**: 42.6% of CPCN 2024 attendees re-registered for CPCN 2025
2. **Returning Visitor Performance**: +5.1 pp higher visitor-level hit rate validates registration campaign strategy
3. **New Visitor Engagement Lift**: +3.7 pp higher session attendance for new visitors with recommendations
4. **High Recommendation Coverage**: 89.3% of registered visitors received personalized conversion campaign recommendations
5. **Strong Show Attendance**: 87.4% of registered visitors physically attended the event

### ⚠️ Areas for Improvement

1. **Lost Registration Campaign Signal**: Critical sessions lost 96-99% of registration recommendations in conversion campaign
2. **Session Engagement Gap**: Only 34.1% of returning visitors attended sessions vs 37.6% of new visitors
3. **Late Registrant Gap**: 228 visitors (10.7%) did not receive recommendations
4. **Cancelled Sessions Impact**: 30.7% of registration campaign recommendations were for sessions that didn't happen

### 🎯 Recommendations

1. **Cross-Validate Algorithms**: Compare registration and conversion campaign recommendations to identify drift before events
2. **Carry Forward Registration Signal**: Sessions highly recommended in registration campaign should receive conversion campaign boost
3. **Implement Session Status Tracking**: Flag cancelled sessions earlier in the pipeline
4. **Add Popularity Boosting**: Historical popularity from registration campaign is a strong attendance predictor
5. **Continuous Processing**: Eliminate late registrant coverage gaps
6. **Investigate Late Registrant Behavior**: These high-commitment visitors show exceptional engagement
