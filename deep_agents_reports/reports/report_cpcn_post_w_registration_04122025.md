## NEW: Registration Campaign Effectiveness Summary

### Overall Registration Campaign Impact

| Metric | Value | Assessment |
|--------|-------|------------|
| **Registration Pool (CPCN 2024)** | 1,576 | - |
| **Re-registration Rate** | 42.6% (671) | ✅ Good retention |
| **Venue Attendance Rate** | 87.9% (590) | ✅ Excellent |
| **Session Engagement Rate** | 34.1% (229) | ⚠️ Room for improvement |
| **Returning Visitor Hit Rate Lift** | +1.9 pp | ✅ Registration campaign adds value |
| **Lost Registration Signal** | 99.6% for some sessions | 🔴 Critical algorithm issue |

### Conversion Campaign Impact by Visitor Type

| Visitor Type | With Recs Session Rate | No Recs Session Rate | Lift from Recs |
|--------------|:----------------------:|:--------------------:|:--------------:|
| **Returning Visitors** | 33.4% | 47.2% | -13.8 pp |
| **New Visitors** | 38.1% | 34.4% | **+3.7 pp** |

**Key Finding:** Recommendations appear to have a positive impact on session attendance for **new visitors** (+3.7 pp), while returning visitors without recommendations showed unusually high engagement (possibly late-registering highly committed attendees).

### Recommendations for Future Events

#### 1. **Cross-Validate Recommendations Across Modes**
- Compare registration campaign and conversion campaign recommendations to identify drift
- Flag sessions with >50% recommendation drop for manual review

#### 2. **Implement Popularity Boosting**
- Sessions like "Chronic insomnia" (796 reg recs → 3 conv recs → 84 attendance) show massive under-recommendation
- Add popularity signal from registration campaign to conversion campaign algorithm

#### 3. **Track Session Status Changes**
- Monitor for cancelled sessions after registration campaign recommendations
- 4,834 registration recs (30.7%) were for sessions that didn't happen as planned

#### 4. **Leverage Registration Data for Conversion Campaign**
- Sessions highly recommended in registration campaign should receive a boost in conversion campaign
- Historical popularity is a strong predictor of future attendance

#### 5. **Investigate Late Registrant Behavior**
- Late registrants (no recommendations) show higher session engagement
- Consider continuous processing to capture these high-commitment visitors
