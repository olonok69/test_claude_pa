## Key Insights

### ✅ Strengths

1. **High Recommendation Coverage**: 89.3% of registered visitors received personalized recommendations
2. **Strong Show Attendance**: 87.4% of registered visitors physically attended the event
3. **Consistent Engagement**: Both groups showed similar session attendance rates (~36%)

### ⚠️ Areas for Improvement

1. **No Engagement Lift**: Recommendations did not increase session attendance (+0.1 pp vs +21.1 pp for LVS)
2. **Low Hit Rate**: Only 15.3% of visitors with recommendations attended a recommended session
3. **Coverage Gap**: 228 visitors (10.7%) did not receive recommendations (late registrants)
4. **Opportunity**: 1,613 visitors received recommendations but didn't attend any recommended sessions

### 🎯 Recommendations

1. **Improve relevance**: Analyze why 84.7% of visitors didn't attend recommended sessions
2. **Add popularity boosting**: Capture universally appealing sessions the similarity algorithm misses
3. **Implement continuous processing**: Eliminate the 10.7% late registrant coverage gap
4. **Investigate low session attendance**: Only 36.5% of visitors attended any sessions (vs 78.6% at LVS)
