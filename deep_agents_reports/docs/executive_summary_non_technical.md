# Deep Agents Show Report – Business Overview

Our show report application automatically prepares a comprehensive view of how an event is shaping up **before doors open**.
It turns the data we already collect (registrations, demographics, past attendance, planned sessions) into a clear, narrative report for decision‑makers.

In practical terms, it answers questions like:

- Are we attracting the right audience segments?
- Which sessions look most critical or at risk?
- How does this year compare with previous editions?
- Where should sales, marketing, and operations focus in the final weeks?

Instead of analysts manually pulling numbers and building slides, the system runs a single command and produces a ready‑to‑read report.

---

## What It Delivers

- **Executive summary:** A plain‑language overview of key trends, risks, and opportunities.
- **Key metrics:** Registration volume, audience mix, new vs. returning visitors, and session demand.
- **Segment insights:** How important customer groups (e.g. roles, regions, specialties) are engaging with the event.
- **Actionable flags:** Sessions or segments that look under‑served, over‑subscribed, or mis‑aligned with our goals.

The result is a **pre‑show “health check”** that leadership can review in one sitting.

---

## How It Works (Conceptually)

1. **Connects to our existing data**  
   It reads the event database that already stores registrations, past attendance, and the session programme.

2. **Follows a fixed checklist**  
   A predefined list of questions (for example "Who are we missing?", "Which sessions will be bottlenecks?") is baked into the system.  
   Every run answers the same questions in the same order, so reports are comparable across events and years.

3. **Uses AI to analyse and write**  
   An AI assistant is given strict instructions:
   - It may only use our real data.
   - It must save all tables and numbers into files.
   - It then writes a clear narrative around those facts.

4. **Packages everything into a report**  
   The final output is a structured report document, alongside supporting tables and charts that analysts can explore further if needed.

---

## Why It Matters for Leadership

- **Speed:** Turnaround moves from days of manual work to **hours or less**.
- **Consistency:** Every show is evaluated using the **same lens**, enabling fair comparison and portfolio‑level views.
- **Confidence:** Numbers are traceable back to saved files; if a figure looks surprising, we can drill down to the exact data behind it.
- **Focus:** The report highlights **where to act**, not just what happened—helping C‑level teams prioritise marketing pushes, programme tweaks, and resourcing.

---

## Role of AI (In Plain Terms)

AI here is **a smart assistant, not an autonomous decision‑maker**:

- It does the heavy lifting of reading large amounts of data and drafting text.
- It is constrained by a pre‑approved checklist and our data sources.
- Everything it produces can be reviewed, checked, and re‑run.

This keeps us **in control of the decisions**, while using AI to dramatically reduce the time from data to insight.
